Business is important, we're not going to lie to ourselves or to you by saying otherwise, however, **creativity and its exploration are equally important**, which is why we have two main initiatives, one with more history than us since it was born several years before and one that was born alongside us.

The first is [Creatyum](https://creatyum.media), a blog and podcast about design and creativity divulgation, started by [Alejandro Mártir](https://alemartir.com) in October 2014, and the second is our "playground" [AM25 Labs](https://labs.am25.work), where the exploration goes beyond software solutions and tools.
